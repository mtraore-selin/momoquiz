How does GMDT Quiz work?

1. Create Quiz

Users can effortlessly create a quiz or learning game on any subject. The process is quick, taking only a few minutes.

2. Host a Challenge

Once the quiz is prepared, hosts can initiate a challenge with Quiz questions and share it with remote players.

3. Join with PIN

Participants can join the challenge by entering the unique PIN provided by the host.

4. Answer on Your Device

Using their own devices, players can respond to the quiz questions.

5. Track Participants' Results

GMDT Quiz offers the ability to track participants' results, enabling hosts to monitor their progress and performance.
